## Tmux

* **tmux** ---> to insert
* **exit** ---> to exit
* **CTRL^b %** ---> to open verticaly |
* **CTRL^b "** ---> to open horizontally -
* **ctrl^b c** ---> to open new window
* **ctrl^b p** ---> to go to the previous window
* **ctrl^b n** ---> to go to the next window
* **CTRL^b ,** ---> to rename the window
